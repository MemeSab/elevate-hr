Impact in Practice

Real-world interventions that moved the needle for major organisations.

Local Authority

Cornwall Council

A complex local authority. A culture that needed more than a workshop. We ran a diagnostic-first OD capacity programme that gave Cornwall's leaders the tools, language, and confidence to drive change from the inside. The impact was independently evaluated.

Public Sector

NW ADASS Antiracism Programme

Anti-racist leadership isn't a training day. It's a sustained shift in how power, accountability, and practice work across a system. This locality-based programme reached leaders across Bolton, Trafford, Oldham, Sefton, and beyond — moving the conversation from awareness into action.

Frontline Delivery

Tower Hamlets

Frontline practitioners who coach well change the culture around them. This programme built coaching capability at the point of delivery — giving people the skills and confidence to have the conversations that matter, every day.
